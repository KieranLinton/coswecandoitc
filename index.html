Papa Gumbo

About

Papa Gumbo is a DJ and Producer that has performed on many stages at small and large festivals across europe over the last 25 years.
Being a big fan of DanceHall has led him on to good vibe Ragga Jungle and Drum & Bass where he packs out dancefloor.
He also produces his own music and has a more based techno live show with a conscious message.
Along with this he is part of the Renegade Radio crew, where he has a regular show.
